About IPL

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India contested during April and May of every year by eight teams representing different cities or regions in India.

Established in 2008 by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), the IPL has become one of the most popular and lucrative sports leagues globally, attracting top cricketing talent from around the world.
